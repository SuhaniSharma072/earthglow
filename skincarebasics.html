Skincare Basics

1. Know Your Skin Type

Oily: Shiny, acne-prone, larger pores

Dry: Flaky, tight, rough texture

Combination: Oily in T-zone, dry elsewhere

Sensitive: Easily irritated, red, or itchy

2. Cleanse Twice Daily

Use a gentle cleanser with lukewarm water

Avoid harsh soaps that strip natural oils

3. Moisturize

Keeps skin hydrated and balanced

Choose light moisturizers for oily skin, rich creams for dry skin

4. Sun Protection

Apply SPF 30+ sunscreen every day

Prevents premature aging, hyperpigmentation, and sunburn

5. Exfoliate

Removes dead skin cells for a brighter complexion

Use gentle scrubs or chemical exfoliants (AHA/BHA)

6. Eye Care

Use eye cream or lightweight moisturizer around the eyes

Helps prevent dryness and dark circles

7. Healthy Habits

Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet

Remove makeup before bed

Avoid touching your face frequently

Tip: A simple routine to start with:

Morning: Cleanser → Moisturizer → Sunscreen

Night: Cleanser → Moisturizer
