Aloe Vera & Honey Soothing Gel

Ingredients: 1 tsp aloe vera gel + ½ tsp honey

How to use: Apply and leave on for 15 minutes, then rinse.

Benefits:

Aloe vera → cools, reduces redness, and helps repair UV damage.

Honey → locks in moisture and speeds healing.

Yogurt & Orange Peel Powder

Ingredients: 1 tsp yogurt + ½ tsp dried orange peel powder

How to use: Mix, apply 10 minutes, rinse.

Benefits:

Orange peel → contains vitamin C, helps fade pigmentation.

Yogurt → soothes and brightens skin naturally.

Turmeric & Yogurt Face Mask

Ingredients: ½ tsp turmeric + 2 tsp plain yogurt

How to use: Apply to clean face for 10–15 minutes, rinse with lukewarm water.

Benefits:

Turmeric → reduces dark spots, evens skin tone.

Yogurt → contains lactic acid, gently exfoliates and hydrates.
