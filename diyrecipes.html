Chapped Lips

Chapped lips happen when the skin on the lips becomes dry, cracked, or irritated. They are often caused by cold weather, wind, sun exposure, dehydration, or frequent licking of the lips.

Pimples

Pimples are small, inflamed spots on the skin that occur when hair follicles become clogged with oil, dead skin cells, and bacteria. They often appear on the face, back, or chest and can be red, swollen, and sometimes filled with pus.
