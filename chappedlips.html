Honey and Sugar Scrub

Ingredients: 1 tsp honey + 1 tsp brown sugar

How to use: Mix and gently massage onto lips for 1–2 minutes, then rinse.

Benefits:

Honey → natural humectant (draws moisture in), antibacterial (prevents infection if lips are cracked), and healing.

Sugar → gentle exfoliator that removes dead, flaky skin, allowing moisture to penetrate better.

Plain Butter Overnight

Ingredients: A small amount of unsalted butter

How to use: Dab on lips before bed, leave overnight.

Benefits:

Deeply hydrates and softens dry skin.

Heals cracks and prevents further moisture loss.
